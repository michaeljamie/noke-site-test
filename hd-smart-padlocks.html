WITHOUT LOCK POWER?

Nokē Jump-start Feature:

In the rare event that you lose lock power, this allows you to touch an external 9V battery to the base of the lock to give your lock a jump-start. Once you're jumped, lift the shackle to open the lock.

PHONE DIE OR WITHOUT ONE?

Nokē Quick-Click Feature:

Any user who may not have a smart device can simply use the touch sensor to tap in a series of short and long taps using a custom code.
