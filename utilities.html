NOKĒ IN

UTILITIES

Across the globe millions of utility companies use billions of locks daily. Controlling these locks, staying compliant with safety and security regulations, and keeping assets secure is time consuming, expensive and frustrating.

Nokē is transforming, streamlining, and simplifying access control in the utility industry on gates, entry points, tool cabinets, valves, lockers, vehicles, and beyond. Technicians open locks from their phones, managers have 100% visibility and control of the locks from anywhere and everyone feels the benefits of the Nokē smartlocking security platform.
