THE MOTION SENSOR
KEEPS THE BAD GUYS OUT

AND THE GOOD GUYS SAFE

The Nokē In-Wall Door Controller has a built-in front-facing motion sensor allowing for:

NOTIFICATIONS

Receive notice of movement in front of and behind the door

AUTO WAKE

Automatic waking of controller in battery-only applicatiions

HEAT MAPS

Display areas of your facility that experience the most traffic

VIRTUAL PERIMETER

Usage of controller to create a virtual perimeter around your facility

BEHIND DOOR DETECTION

Optional sensors to monitor behind door motion allowing automatic unlocking from the inside when motion is sensed
