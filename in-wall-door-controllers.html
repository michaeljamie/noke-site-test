INTRODUCING THE NOKĒ IN-WALL SERIES

This keyless, sleek, thin, and modern access controller puts you in control who's coming and going, no matter where you are.

IN-WALL SERIES

The Nokē In-Wall Controller combined with the simple Nokē management software and mobile app, creates a high security, connected, physical access system. The device directly connects to any locking mechanism and is opened from your smart device.
