THE FEATURES

Nokē SMART LOTO combines the strength of Nokē hardware with the intelligence
of the Nokē SMART LOTO App and Web Portal to bring you the smartest loto procedures ever designed.

Eliminate management of traditional locks and keys

Add and remove lockouts from any smart device

Quickly create, edit, approve, and close job orders

Receive cloud-based, real-time data and audit trails

Quickly alert crews of any job site safety hazards

Rapidly notify and assign technicians

Easily purchase replacement parts and communicate with manufacturers

Provide up-to-date and accurate isolation processes

Integrate with programs that ensure personnel have necessary qualifications to work on equipment

Eliminate conventional paperwork that easily gets overlooked and misplaced
