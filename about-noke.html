WHO IS NOKĒ

Founded in 2014, Nokē is a Utah-based, leading-edge tech company, revolutionizing the physical security industry with its transformative smartlock hardware and software solutions.

Nokē is combining innovation with technology to create purposeful, stronger, smarter, sustainable, physical security solutions that provide businesses with peace of mind, ease, improved safety and a modernized, security solution.
